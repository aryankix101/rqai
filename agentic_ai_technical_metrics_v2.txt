Technical Indicator Metrics Schema for the Technical Module (v2)

Trend & Momentum Indicators (Price Direction and Speed)

Price Momentum (Returns)
• mom_1m, mom_3m, mom_6m (primary momentum horizons)
• mom_12m (secondary; lower weight for weekly horizon)
All momentum values should be normalized (z-score or percentile).

Moving Averages and Trend Structure
• ma50, ma200
• price_above_ma50 (bool)
• price_above_ma200 (bool)
• golden_cross_recent, death_cross_recent
• higher_highs_lows (categorical: uptrend, downtrend, mixed)

MACD
• macd_line
• macd_signal
• macd_histogram (primary MACD input)
• macd_bull_cross, macd_bear_cross (optional events)

RSI (Stretch / Exhaustion Only)
• rsi_14d
• rsi_overbought, rsi_oversold (optional, not used as direct signals)

ADX (Trend Strength)
• adx_14d (e.g., >25 = trending)



Volatility and Risk Indicators (Fluctuation and Risk Profile)

Realized Volatility and Regime Inputs
• realized_vol_21d
• realized_vol_252d
• vol_ratio_21v252 
• Optional: realized_vol_63d smoothing
Volatility regime is derived:
vol_regime ∈ {high, neutral, low}

Optional Implied Volatility
• IV_rank (or option IV percentile)

Beta and Correlation
• beta_1y
• corr_sp500
• r2_sp500

Risk-Adjusted Performance
• sharpe_1y
• sortino_1y

Drawdowns
• max_drawdown_1y

ATR
• atr_14d (absolute or percent)



Volume and Liquidity Indicators (Activity and Executability)

Liquidity and Executability (Hard Filters)
• avg_vol_3m (shares)
• avg_dollar_vol_21d
• turnover_pct_21 (optional)
• roll_spread_21
• cs_spread_21 (Corwin-Schultz spread)
• liquidity_score_0_100 (composite)

Volume Confirmation (Signal Support)
• vol_last
• volume_spike_z (Z-score normalized)
• obv_trend_21d (up, down, flat)
• acc_dist_trend_21d (up, down, flat)



Price Pattern & Event Indicators
Gap Events
• gap_up, gap_down
• gap_size_pct

52-Week Extremes
• new_52w_high
• new_52w_low
• pct_from_52w_high (optional)
• pct_from_52w_low (optional)

Structure
• higher_highs
• higher_lows
• prior_swing_break_up
• prior_swing_break_down

Regime and Volatility Shifts
• vol_regime 



Relative Performance & Comparative Metrics 
Relative Strength vs Index
• vs_sp500_3m
• vs_sp500_6m
• vs_sp500_12m
• rel_strength_trend (rising, flat, falling)

Sector and Industry Comparison
• rel_strength_sector_3m
• sector_rank_percentile

Capture Ratios
• up_capture_1y
• down_capture_1y

Optional
• corr_to_factor_x
• r2_to_factor_x
• seasonality_score
• hurst
